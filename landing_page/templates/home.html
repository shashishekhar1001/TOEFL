TOEFL iBT Test Structure.

TOEFL ibt is 4.30 hours long including the check-in. It's divided into four sections "Reading", "Listening", "Speaking", "Writing" and a break of 10 minutes after two sections.


Reading
  • alarm 60-80 minutes
  • question_answer 36- 56 Questions
  • description 3 or 4 passages.
Listening
  • alarm 60-90 minutes
  • question_answer 34- 51 Questions
  • description Listen lectures then QnA.
Speaking
  • alarm 20 minutes
  • question_answer 6 Tasks
  • description Express Opinion.
Writing
  • alarm 50 minutes
  • question_answer 2 Tasks
  • description Write essay.
